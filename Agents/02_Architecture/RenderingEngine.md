# Rendering Engine

Status: TEMPLATE — Architect must complete during Sprint 0.

## Required views

- full arrangement;
- harmonic roadmap;
- isolated roles;
- separated and combined hands;
- learning chunks;
- arbitrary excerpts.

## Required visible semantics

- beat and subdivision location;
- section and idea structure;
- repetition and alternate endings;
- canonical harmony;
- bass, inversion, and voicing distinction;
- required, suggested, optional, unspecified, and unknown states;
- subordinate familiar-shape hints;
- lyric alignment without whitespace hacks.

## Non-goals for Prototype 1

- engraving-quality score layout;
- final notation symbol system;
- graphical editing;
- automatic pagination perfection.
