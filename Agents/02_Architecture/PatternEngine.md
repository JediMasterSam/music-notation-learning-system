# Pattern Engine

Status: TEMPLATE — Architect must complete during Sprint 0.

## Required behaviors

- reusable built-in and user-defined pattern definitions;
- canonical musical name, aliases, and exemplar references;
- applicable roles;
- typed parameters;
- explicit overrides and exceptions;
- deterministic expansion;
- provenance from expanded events to pattern and overrides;
- vocabulary reporting;
- transposition behavior;
- no admission of a new shared pattern solely to shorten one fixture.

## Architecture questions to resolve

- distinction between compositional pattern and rendering shorthand;
- whether pattern output is event-based, role-based, or both;
- parameter validation;
- nested composition;
- override precedence;
- handling of hand assignment;
- preservation of intentionally unspecified detail;
- corpus evidence required for shared-library admission.
