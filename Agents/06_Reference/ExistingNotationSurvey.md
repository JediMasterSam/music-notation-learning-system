# Existing Notation Survey

Status: To be completed.

Compare at minimum:

- traditional staff notation;
- chord sheets and lead sheets;
- Nashville Number System;
- Roman numeral analysis;
- figured bass;
- tablature;
- piano-roll representations;
- pattern or groove notation;
- MusicXML and MIDI as interchange formats.

For each, record:

- what it communicates well;
- what it omits;
- cognitive burden for the target learner;
- reusable concepts;
- unsuitable assumptions;
- potential interoperability value.
