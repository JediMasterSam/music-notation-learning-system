# Research Register

Use this document to record external research questions, sources, findings, and product implications.

Initial topics:

- cognitive load in music reading and learning;
- lead sheets, Nashville numbers, figured bass, tablature, and existing hybrid notations;
- MusicXML and MIDI interoperability;
- harmonic upper structures and slash-chord pedagogy;
- representation of repetition and variation;
- accessible SVG and music interfaces.

Research findings do not become product requirements automatically. Proposed changes must enter the Product Owner decision process.
