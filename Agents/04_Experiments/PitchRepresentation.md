# Pitch Representation Experiment

Status: Required; unresolved.

## Competing strategies

- absolute pitch;
- key-relative scale degree;
- chord-relative pitch;
- hybrid.

## Minimum fixtures

- melody only;
- changing harmony;
- chromatic non-chord tones;
- repeated pattern;
- transposition to at least two keys.

## Architecture requirement

No downstream package may assume one pitch encoding directly. The architect must define an interface or intermediate semantic representation that permits comparison.
