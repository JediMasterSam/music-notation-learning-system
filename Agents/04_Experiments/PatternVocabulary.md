# Pattern Vocabulary Experiment

Status: Required.

## Question

What is the smallest reusable vocabulary that expresses representative accompaniment and bass behavior without forcing note-by-note expansion or proliferating song-specific pattern names?

## Admission test

Before admitting a shared pattern:

1. attempt direct events;
2. attempt composition of existing patterns;
3. attempt parameterization;
4. compare at least two corpus examples;
5. measure learner recognition cost;
6. request Product Owner approval.
